Travel Tips

Getting to Ondo City

Ondo City is accessible by road from major cities. The nearest airport is Akure Airport (AKF), about 45 minutes away. We recommend booking accommodation in advance, especially during the wedding weekend.

Transportation

We are arranging shuttle services from major hotels to the venue. Local taxis and ride-sharing apps are available. We'll provide detailed transportation information to all confirmed guests.

Booking

We recommend booking your accommodation as soon as possible. Contact the hotels directly or through online booking platforms. Group rates may be available - ask about wedding guest discounts.
