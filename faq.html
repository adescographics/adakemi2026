Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know

What time should I arrive at the venue?

▼

We recommend arriving by 7:30 AM for the Traditional Wedding (8:00 AM start) and by 9:30 AM for the Church Wedding (10:00 AM start). This gives everyone time to settle in and enjoy some refreshments before the ceremonies begin.

What is the dress code?

▼

Our wedding colours are Emerald, Gold, Mint Green, and White. We kindly request that all guests dress in one or a combination of these colours. This creates a beautiful and cohesive look in our photographs and makes our celebration even more special.

Can I bring a guest or plus-one?

▼

Please RSVP with the number of guests you'll be bringing. For specific questions about bringing additional guests, kindly contact one of our coordinators listed on the contact page.

Is there a reception or reception dinner?

▼

Yes! After the ceremonies, we will have a reception with light refreshments, live entertainment, and an exquisite dining experience. All guests are invited to stay and celebrate with us.

Where can I find accommodation near the venue?

▼

Visit our Accommodations page for a list of nearby hotels and lodging options in Ondo City. We've curated options for different budgets and preferences.

How do I RSVP?

▼

You can RSVP by calling or WhatsApp our coordinators at the numbers listed on the contact page, or by completing the RSVP form on the Wedding Details page. Please let us know your attendance status and number of guests.

What are the gift options?

▼

We have several gifting options available: cash gifts, a curated gift registry, or physical gifts. You can view all details and account information on our Gifting page.

Can I take photographs during the ceremony?

▼

While we appreciate your enthusiasm, we kindly ask that you refrain from taking photographs during the ceremonies. We have professional photographers capturing these special moments. However, feel free to take photos during the reception!

What if I have dietary restrictions?

▼

Please inform us of any dietary restrictions when you RSVP. Contact our wedding coordinators to let us know about allergies, vegetarian, vegan, or any other dietary requirements so we can make appropriate arrangements.

Will there be transportation from the hotel to the venue?

▼

Yes, we are arranging shuttle services from major hotels in Ondo City to the venue. More details will be sent to confirmed guests. For specific questions, please contact our coordinators.
